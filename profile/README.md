### A Note on closed-source Tooling

You will notice that the main applications are closed-source. This is a deliberate choice to protect the original developers, [**Velan Studios**](https://www.velanstudios.com/).

> Velan Studios still actively uses their custom engine (Viper) for other and future titles. To avoid causing them any harm or accidentally exposing any of their internal specifications to the public, I am keeping the primary tools closed-source.
> 
> I am fully aware that this will slow down development of new things dramatically but currently I have not found a way to protect Velan Studios and allow for contribution by others. This might change in the future but can't promise anything for certain.
>
> I appreciate your understanding.
